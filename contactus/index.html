About Palmmicro
  
Palmmicro provides modules and chips with complete hardware and software reference design for video and voice communication. Our target customers are device manufacturers, but end users can also find documents, software downloads and useful development tools in our web site too.
 
Overview
When: We started business since Jan, 2006, after Centrality (original Palmmicro) discontinued its VoIP business.
Where: Palmmicro office is located in Shenzhen, a southern city in China.
Who: Palmmicro is a privately owned company, founders include many PA1688 developers like Woody.
What we do: We sell modules and chips for low cost and high performance video and voice communication hardware device. Usually we provide customers with common standard designs without NRE charges nor special software license. We also provide special hardware and software design service based on our solution platform with a development fee.
